---

## Product Vision

### The Problem
People with dietary restrictions or specific health goals suffer from **"decision fatigue"** and manual tracking errors, leading to abandoned health journeys.

### The Solution
**NutriTrack AI** is a smart meal-planning platform that automates recipe discovery based on health profiles, real-time pantry inventory, and caloric goals.

---

## Key Features (Initial Backlog)

* **Health Profiler:** Set allergies, dietary labels (Keto, Vegan, etc.), and macro targets.
* **AI Recipe Engine:** Fetches recipes from a 3rd-party API that strictly match user constraints.
* **Pantry Inventory Manager:** Track what’s in your fridge to reduce food waste.
* **Interactive Macro Dashboard:** Weekly visual reports on protein, carb, and fat intake.
* **Smart Grocery List:** Automatically generates a shopping list based on the week's meal plan.

### User Personas
1.  **The Performance Athlete:** Needs high-protein meal automation for training cycles.
2.  **The Allergy-Conscious User:** Needs 100% certainty that recipes are gluten/nut-free.
